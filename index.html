About Me

I am from Venezuela and I have always been connected to the sea and water sports like swimming and surfing. On weekends I used to go surfing in La Guaira, near Caracas. My interest in art runs in my family: my father was a goldsmith and my brother is an actor. Since I was a child, I have been passionate about photography, a world I discovered when my father gave me my first camera.

Although I have been involved in art since I was a child, it wasn't until I was 26 years old that I decided to study filmmaking, after almost four years studying history. After graduating, my brother suggested I move to Canada. Now I'm here, studying IMD, facing new challenges and exciting learning.
